Our Story

Built on the Corner of Craft & Character

District 7 started with one chair, one mirror and a simple belief: every man deserves to leave the chair standing a little taller. We blend the discipline of old-school barbering — straight razors, hot towels, and an honest conversation — with the precision of modern styling.

No apps rushing you out. No cookie-cutter cuts. Just skilled barbers who take pride in the details and treat your chair time like it matters. Because it does.

  • Master barbers with 15+ years behind the chair
  • Premium products & sanitized tools, every cut
  • A shop that feels like your kind of place
